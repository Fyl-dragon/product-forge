# PF Research

Use research to drive decisions, not to write generic summaries.

## Workflow

1. Clarify research question and target decision.
2. Inspect local context first; browse only when latest or external facts are required.
3. Compare competitors or alternatives by product-relevant axes:
   - target users
   - workflow
   - information architecture
   - pricing/package
   - permissions
   - integration model
   - operational capabilities
   - strengths and weaknesses
4. Convert findings into implications for roadmap, PRD, prototype, and risks.
5. Save or propose `.product/features/<feature-slug>/01-research.md`.
6. Recommend `$pf-roadmap` when the research changes priority, sequencing, or product strategy.

## Output

Use Chinese. Include a comparison matrix, actionable insights, and explicit product decisions or options.
