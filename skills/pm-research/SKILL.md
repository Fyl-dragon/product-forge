# PM Research

Use research to drive decisions, not to write generic summaries.

## Workflow

1. Read `../product-forge/references/interaction-protocol.md` and follow it strictly.
2. Before researching, ask exactly one boundary question with exactly three options:
   - 问题：这次 research 要支持什么决策？
   - A. 竞品/替代方案对比，决定做不做和怎么区分。
   - B. 用户场景判断，决定优先支持哪类流程。
   - C. 方案取舍判断，决定保留哪些能力和风险。
   - 请回复 A / B / C。
3. After the user chooses, clarify research question and target decision.
4. Inspect local context first; browse only when latest or external facts are required.
5. Compare competitors or alternatives by product-relevant axes:
   - target users
   - workflow
   - information architecture
   - pricing/package
   - permissions
   - integration model
   - operational capabilities
   - strengths and weaknesses
6. Convert findings into implications for roadmap, PRD, prototype, and risks.
7. Save or propose only `.product/features/<feature-slug>/01-research.md`.
8. Recommend `$pm-roadmap` when the research changes priority, sequencing, or product strategy.

## Output

Use Chinese. Include a comparison matrix, actionable insights, and explicit product decisions or options.
