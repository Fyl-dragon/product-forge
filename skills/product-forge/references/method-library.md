# ProductForge Method Library

Use this as the external-method reference map when improving ProductForge workflows.

## Continuous Discovery And Opportunity Trees

Source: Product Talk, Opportunity Solution Trees.

Adopted into ProductForge:

- `$pf-discovery`
- `.product/DISCOVERY.md`
- `opportunity-solution-tree.md`
- outcome, opportunity, solution, assumption test, evidence quality, and decision rule

Reference: https://www.producttalk.org/opportunity-solution-trees/

## Prioritization And Roadmap

Source: Atlassian, prioritization frameworks including RICE.

Adopted into ProductForge:

- `$pf-roadmap`
- `.product/PRIORITIZATION.md`
- reach, impact, confidence, effort, strategic fit, and tradeoff rationale

Reference: https://www.atlassian.com/agile/product-management/prioritization-framework

## North Star And Product Metrics

Source: Amplitude, North Star Framework.

Adopted into ProductForge:

- `$pf-metrics`
- `.product/METRICS.md`
- North Star metric, input metrics, guardrails, baseline, target, owner, launch metrics, and review cadence

Reference: https://amplitude.com/books/north-star/about-north-star-framework

## Working Backwards PRFAQ

Source: Working Backwards PR/FAQ.

Adopted into ProductForge:

- `$pf-narrative`
- `PRFAQ.md`
- press release, customer FAQ, internal FAQ, risks, rejected alternatives, and open questions

Reference: https://workingbackwards.com/resources/working-backwards-pr-faq/

## Project Management Cadence

Adopted into ProductForge:

- `$pf-project`
- project plan
- RAID escalation rules
- stakeholder update cadence and decision requests
- decision log
- launch readiness gate
- acceptance progress state tracker

Boundary: project management artifacts stay product-side and do not become development execution tasks.
